# Placement Cell Guidelines

## 1. Eligibility Criteria
To register with the College Placement Cell, students must satisfy the following:
- Minimum **6.5 CGPA** with no standing backlogs (history of backlogs is permitted by some companies but active backlogs are prohibited).
- Minimum **75% attendance** in all academic and placement-specific training programs.

## 2. Dress Code & Ethics
- **Dress Code**: Professional formal wear (light-colored shirts, dark trousers, necktie, and formal shoes) is mandatory for all recruitment rounds.
- **One Student One Job Policy**: Once a student secures a placement offer with a package exceeding a threshold, they are excluded from further drives, unless it is a "Dream Option" company offering at least 1.5x of the current offer.
- **No-Show Penalty**: Registering for a drive and failing to appear without 24-hour prior approval from the placement officer will lead to suspension from the subsequent three drives.
