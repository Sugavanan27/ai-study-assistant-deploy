# University College Handbook 2026

## 1. Code of Conduct
All students are expected to maintain high standards of academic integrity and personal behavior. Disruption of classes, damage to property, and academic dishonesty (plagiarism/cheating) will lead to disciplinary actions, up to suspension or expulsion.

## 2. Grading System
The college follows a 10-point Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) system:
- **O (Outstanding)**: 10 points (90-100%)
- **A+ (Excellent)**: 9 points (80-89%)
- **A (Very Good)**: 8 points (70-79%)
- **B (Good)**: 7 points (60-69%)
- **C (Pass)**: 6 points (50-59%)
- **F (Fail)**: Less than 50%

## 3. Attendance Policy
Students must maintain a minimum of **75% attendance** in each course. A shortage of attendance down to 65% may be condoned by the Dean on medical grounds with valid documentation. Students below 65% will not be allowed to sit for end-semester exams and must repeat the course.
