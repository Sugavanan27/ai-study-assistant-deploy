# University Library Rules & Regulations

## 1. Membership & Borrowing Limits
- **Students**: Can borrow up to 4 books for a duration of 14 days.
- **Faculty**: Can borrow up to 10 books for a duration of 30 days.
- **Renewals**: Books may be renewed once for an additional 14 days if there are no pending reservations.

## 2. Timings
- **Weekdays**: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM
- **Saturdays**: 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- **Sundays & Holidays**: Closed

## 3. Fines & Penalties
- Overdue books incur a fine of **Rs. 5 per day** per book.
- Loss or damage of books must be reported immediately. The borrower must replace the book or pay triple the current market cost of the book.
- Silence must be observed. Using mobile phones for calls in the reading area is strictly prohibited.
